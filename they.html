"Uralskie pelmeny" is a Russian boy band from Yekaterinburg, has 11 members: Slavka Myasnikov, Andre Rozhkov, Dmitri Brekotnik and 6 Sergeev Isaevs.

In 2017, Yurieva Ilana, a.k.a. "my inner scream", joined the group. The group is positioned as a performer of Armenian music.
